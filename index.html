Black Lives Matter Global Network Launches New MLK Artist Series

On Monday, Black Lives Matter Global Network will launch its newest arts and culture endeavor centering life, love, joy, rest, and pleasure. This series will be on the BLM website and Instagram. Due to the events that happened on January 6th, We need to amplify his work more than ever. We must uplift our community and these artists have been curated as love is a focal point of their work, and with love brings healing and the ability to rejoice and continue the necessary work to move forward as a people.
