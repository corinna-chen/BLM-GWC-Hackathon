Breonna Taylor

Protesters fill streets outside kentucky derby, calling for justice for Breonna Taylor, a 26 year old unarmed Black woman that was killed by Louisville police at her apartment. Brett Hankison, one of three officers involved in the shooting, was fired from the Louisville Metro Police Department on June 23. The other two officers have been placed on administrative leave
